Heterogeneous AI Architecture

Our research demonstrates that Small Language Models (SLMs) outperform Large Language Models (LLMs) for most agentic tasks while delivering 10-30x cost savings. We deploy specialized SLMs for routine operations—parsing, tool calling, summarization—while reserving LLMs for complex reasoning and open-ended dialogue.

Our position paper "Small Language Models are the Future of Agentic AI" outlines the technical and economic advantages of heterogeneous model architectures.

Why SLMs Excel at Agentic AI Tasks

Small Language Models (SLMs) are ideal for agentic systems because each step uses only a narrow slice of LLM capability—parsing commands, producing structured JSON, or summarizing context—tasks perfectly suited to compact, specialized models.
