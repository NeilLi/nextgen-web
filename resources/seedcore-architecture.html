Architecture

The policy layer between the model and the machine.

SeedCore's architecture is designed around event ingestion, policy-governed routing, execution playback, and exception recovery in physical environments.

Core ClaimThe runtime boundary is the product.

Once that boundary is owned, physical AI becomes safer, more observable, and more operationally credible.

Architecture Principles

What the control plane must do.

EventizationNormalize sensor, vision, telemetry, and operator input into one mission stream.

Control begins with coherent event surfaces.

GovernanceEvaluate safety contracts and access boundaries before dispatch.

Execution authority must be explicit.

RecoveryRecord playback and route exceptions without losing mission context.

Infrastructure has to survive real operational variance.
