Architecture

The zero-trust boundary between AI judgment and controlled action.

SeedCore's architecture is designed around event ingress, Governed Agent routing, policy enforcement, custody telemetry, and exception recovery in enterprise environments.

Core ClaimThe runtime boundary is the product.

Once that boundary is owned, high-stakes automation becomes safer, more observable, and more operationally credible.

Architecture Principles

What the runtime boundary must do.

EventizationNormalize sensor, vision, telemetry, provenance, and operator input into one mission stream.

Control begins with coherent event surfaces.

Actor hierarchyKeep AI judgment advisory, Governed Agents accountable, and endpoints policy-scoped.

Execution authority must be explicit.

Recovery and custodyRecord playback and route exceptions without losing mission or custody context.

Infrastructure has to survive real operational variance.
