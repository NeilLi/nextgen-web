Trust and Governance

Governance is inside the runtime, not bolted on after action.

SeedCore is designed for environments where AI judgment is advisory, Governed Agents are accountable, and actuator endpoints move only after policy, approval, and custody checks pass.

Trust Model Every action should be explainable, reviewable, recoverable, and attributable.

That principle shapes how SeedCore handles identity, routing, telemetry, custody, and fallback logic.

Control Model

Core zero-trust controls.

Actor SeparationAI judgment, Governed Agents, operators, and actuator endpoints have distinct responsibilities.

SeedCore does not blur interpretation, accountability, and physical execution into one layer.

Policy ContractsExecution is gated through explicit identity, safety, zoning, and release contracts.

Policy is enforced as runtime logic, not advisory prompt text.

Telemetry and CustodyPlayback records the path from event to endpoint response and custody state.

This supports internal audit, partner review, and regulatory investigation.

Deployment Posture

How SeedCore approaches operational trust.

  • Edge-aware enforcementLatency-critical checks can remain close to the endpoint through edge runtime surfaces.
