SeedCore is not an AI agent company.

Mission

Make physical AI safe, operational, and reliable.

Operating Principles

Infrastructure rules, not AI slogans.

Deny by default

Safety, access, and machine contracts are evaluated before execution, not after a model has already acted.

Multimodal first

Telemetry, vision, sensors, and operator input are first-class runtime inputs.

Exception is the product

Real environments break. Recovery loops, fallbacks, and escalation paths are part of the core runtime.

We deploy beside reality and beside the machine rather than waiting on legacy system replacement.
