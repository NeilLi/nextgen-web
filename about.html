Operating Principles

Infrastructure rules, not autonomy theater.

AI is advisory

AI handles interpretation, planning, and telemetry understanding. It does not receive direct execution authority.

Governed Agents are accountable

The action-taking layer is explicit, scoped, and reviewable before any actuator endpoint moves.

Deny by default

Access, zoning, timing, and custody contracts are evaluated before execution, not after a system has already acted.

Exception is the product

Recovery loops, quarantine paths, and step-up approval flows are part of the core runtime.
